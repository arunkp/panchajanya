EDUCATION

Education is an essential human virtue, a necessity of society, basis of good life and sign of freedom. Education is important for integration of separate entities.

HEALTH

A healthy community reflects a sense of mental and physical well being and is the foundation for achieving all other goals. Good health is often taken for granted but is essential for a productive society.

SPIRITUALITY

Spirituality is a broad concept with room for many perspectives. In general, it includes a sense of connection to something bigger than ourselves, and it typically involves a search for meaning in life.

Panchajanya Foundation

Panchajanya Foundation is a not-for-profit foundation with a goal to create and sustain initiatives to contribute to a just, equitable, humane and sustainable society.The Foundation’s work is aimed at enriching and enhancing the capabilities of individuals to achieve well-being and harmony with respect to ‘Akshara’ – knowledge, ‘Arogya’ – health and ‘Adhyatma’ – spiritual and moral values.
