About Panchajanya Foundation

On this auspicious day, June 3, 2012, the foundation is being officially inaugurated to ensure its activities are driven in a structured manner to reach the larger masses in supporting the needy, in addressing the basic necessities like education, health and in understanding the spiritual and philosophical values of life. The foundation aims at providing these facilities to the needy without any differentiation of caste, creed, color or gender. The Foundation does this through integrated approaches along with individuals and volunteers who support the foundation to achieve the key objectives.

Key Objectives of Panchajanya Foundation

  • Identify & recognize talent – ‘Akshara’ supports to educate the needy & capable
  • Identify & support – ‘Arogya’ supports those who are in need of the basic medical care
  • Public communication and advocacy – ‘Adyathma’ intends to inculcate strong spiritual and philosophical values.
